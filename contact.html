Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical web design project take?

The timeline varies depending on the project scope, but most websites take between 4-8 weeks from initial concept to launch.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes, we work with clients from all over the world. We use video calls, project management tools, and collaborative software to ensure smooth communication.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer both project-based pricing and ongoing retainer options. The cost depends on the scope and complexity of your project.

Do you provide website maintenance after launch?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance and support packages to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally.

Can you work with our existing brand guidelines?

Absolutely! We can either work within your existing brand guidelines or help develop new ones if needed.

What platforms do you build websites on?

We work with various platforms including WordPress, Shopify, and custom solutions built with modern frameworks.
